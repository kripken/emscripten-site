Community¶

Welcome to the Emscripten community!

Get in touch¶

The best ways to contact us for support are the mailing list and the IRC channel. Ask questions, share your ideas, or just join the conversation.

  • Mailing list: emscripten-discuss
  • IRC: #emscripten on irc.mozilla.org

Emscripten also has a presence on social media:

  • #emscripten (Emscripten Hashtag on Twitter)
  • @kripken (an Emscripten developer’s account on Twitter, mentions Emscripten updates)

Report a bug¶

Bug reports can be posted in the GitHub Issue Tracker.

Blogs¶

Get the latest official Emscripten news on the blogs: Developer blog (azakai) and Mozilla blog.

In addition, other interesting blogs and demos are regularly shared on Twitter by the development team (@kripken) and the broader community (#emscripten).

Contribute¶

Anyone can contribute to Emscripten. We’ve got some ideas to get you started on our Contributing page.
